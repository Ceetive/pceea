Student run Clubs

As of 2022, the students at the european school of Alicante have had the ability to create their independently student run clubs.

How to create one? Three steps: fill in the proposal document, contact Gaspar Billerault, present your project to the directors.

Entrepreneurship Club

The entrepreneurship club is a hands-on experience where you are exposed to entrepreneurship and innovation by guiding you through the new venture creation process applied to your venture ideas. Through this club you will understand:

The Problem that your customer is facing, the solution, & value of it; create customer Profiles/Personas; create customer Discovery Highlights; identify the competitive Advantage; create a competitor positioning chart; create an MVP and validate Customer Testing Results; create a problem statement; beach customer segment: TAM+SAM+assumptions.

Don’t worry if you don’t have any business experience or a business idea, in the club you will learn it all!
